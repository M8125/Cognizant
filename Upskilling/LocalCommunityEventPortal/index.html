Community Bulletin

The city council is pleased to announce a full calendar of events this season. From the Spring Fair to the Park Cleanup, there's something for every resident. Volunteers are still needed for the Park Cleanup on May 18th - gloves and bags will be provided on site. The Art Walk returns in June with twelve local artists confirmed so far. Food Truck Night will rotate vendors weekly, so check the portal for the latest lineup. As always, registration through the portal helps us plan attendance and parking. Thank you to everyone who volunteered last season - your help made these events possible.
